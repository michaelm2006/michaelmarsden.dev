Gangs in Southampton

Southampton, like many UK cities, has seen the rise and fall of various street gangs over the years. These groups often form around local neighborhoods and have been involved in territorial disputes, sometimes leading to conflicts known as "postcode wars." While the city has worked hard to reduce violence and promote positive opportunities for young people, the stories of these rivalries remain a part of Southampton's urban history. Today, music and creative expression—like that of YS Vince—offer a powerful alternative to street life, helping to inspire and uplift the next generation.
